### Discover the Authentic Taste of Chinese Tea at Ying Ying Tea House in Binondo, Manila

Ying Ying Tea House is a hidden gem located in the heart of Binondo, Manila. If you're a tea lover or looking to experience an authentic Chinese tea experience, then this is the perfect spot for you.

Upon entering Ying Ying Tea House, you'll be welcomed by its cozy and traditional Chinese ambiance. The place is adorned with intricate details and furnishings, such as wooden chairs and tables, paper lanterns, and Chinese artworks, making you feel like you've been transported to China.

One of the highlights of Ying Ying Tea House is their extensive selection of teas, which includes different varieties of green tea, black tea, oolong tea, and even fruit-infused tea. Their teas are imported from China, ensuring that you're getting the authentic taste of Chinese tea.

Aside from their tea selection, they also offer delicious Chinese snacks and dim sum that perfectly complement their teas. You can't go wrong with their popular steamed dumplings, fried spring rolls, and egg tarts, which are all made fresh daily.

In addition to the tea and snacks, the service at Ying Ying Tea House is exceptional. Their staff is knowledgeable about the different types of teas they offer and can help you choose the best one that suits your taste preference.

If you're looking for an authentic Chinese tea experience, then you should definitely visit Ying Ying Tea House in Binondo, Manila. With their extensive tea selection, delicious Chinese snacks, and exceptional service, you'll surely have an unforgettable tea time experience.
