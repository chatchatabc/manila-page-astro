### Kapitolyo

Kapitolyo, a small neighborhood in Pasig City, Manila, has become a popular foodie destination in recent years. With its wide variety of restaurants and cuisines, it has become a must-visit for anyone looking for a unique culinary experience. Here are some of the top restaurants to try in Kapitolyo:

Kanto Freestyle Breakfast - This popular restaurant serves breakfast all day and offers a variety of Filipino and international dishes. Their bestsellers include the Tapsilog (beef tapa with garlic rice and egg) and the Choco Peanut Butter Pancakes.

Locavore - This restaurant offers modern Filipino cuisine with a twist. Their famous Sizzling Sinigang is a must-try, as well as their Lechon and Oyster Sisig.

El Chupacabra - This Mexican restaurant is known for its delicious tacos and margaritas. Their menu also includes other Mexican favorites such as nachos, quesadillas, and burritos.

Silantro Fil-Mex Cantina - Another Mexican restaurant, Silantro Fil-Mex Cantina offers a wide selection of burritos, tacos, and quesadillas. Their Grilled Beef Soft Tacos and Beef Nachos are highly recommended.

Tipsy Pig Gastropub - This gastropub serves an array of international dishes and drinks. Their Crispy Pork Belly and Craft Beers are highly recommended.

These are just a few of the many restaurants in Kapitolyo. With its lively atmosphere and diverse food options, it's no wonder why Kapitolyo has become a go-to destination for foodies in Manila.
