### Wai Ying Fastfood: A Dim Sum Haven in the Heart of Binondo

If you're a foodie looking for an authentic Chinese food experience, then Wai Ying Fastfood in Binondo, Manila should be on your list of must-visit places. Located in the heart of Chinatown, Wai Ying Fastfood has been serving delectable dim sum and other Chinese dishes since 1998.

As you step inside the restaurant, you'll be greeted by a bustling and lively atmosphere. The place is always packed, especially during peak hours, so be prepared to wait for a table. But trust us, the wait is worth it!

One of the highlights of Wai Ying Fastfood is their extensive menu. They offer a wide selection of dim sum, from the classic siomai, hakaw, and steamed buns to the more adventurous fried radish cake and pork and shrimp dumplings. All of their dim sum are freshly made, steaming hot, and bursting with flavor.

Aside from dim sum, Wai Ying Fastfood also serves a variety of Chinese dishes, such as stir-fried noodles, rice toppings, and congee. Their must-try dishes include the Yang Chow Fried Rice, Salt and Pepper Squid, and the Sweet and Sour Pork.

What sets Wai Ying Fastfood apart from other dim sum places is their affordable prices. You can enjoy a filling meal without breaking the bank. Plus, the staff are friendly and accommodating, making the dining experience even more enjoyable.

In summary, Wai Ying Fastfood in Binondo, Manila is a must-visit for anyone who loves dim sum and Chinese cuisine. With its extensive menu, affordable prices, and lively atmosphere, it's no wonder why this place is a favorite among locals and tourists alike. So, the next time you find yourself in Chinatown, make sure to drop by Wai Ying Fastfood for a delicious and satisfying meal.
