## Art in Island: The 3D Art Museum You Need to Visit in Manila

### Introduction

If you're looking for a fun and interactive way to experience art, look no further than Art in Island. Located in the heart of Manila, this unique art museum offers a different approach to appreciating art. Rather than simply admiring the artworks from afar, you can become part of the artwork itself.

<Picture sizes={"320px"} src="/images/places/art-in-island4.jpeg" alt="art1" />

Art in Island features 14 3D galleries that are full of immersive and interactive murals. Each gallery has a different theme, ranging from aquatic life to popular landmarks, so there's something for everyone. The murals are designed to trick your eyes and create the illusion of depth, so you can pose and interact with the art in creative and fun ways.

### Mirror Room and Photography

One of the highlights of Art in Island is the mirror room, where you can create endless reflections and take fun and quirky photos. The museum's staff is always on hand to assist visitors in capturing the perfect shot, and there are plenty of props available to add to the fun.

<Picture sizes={"320px"} src="/images/places/art-in-island2.jpeg" alt="art1" />

### Affordable and Accessible

Art in Island is perfect for families, friends, and even solo travelers looking for an entertaining and Instagram-worthy experience in Manila. The museum's affordable admission fees make it accessible to everyone. It's a great way to spend a few hours, and you're guaranteed to leave with lots of memorable photos.

<Picture sizes={"320px"} src="/images/places/art-in-island5.jpeg" alt="art1" />

### Conclusion

If you're planning a trip to Manila, make sure to add Art in Island to your itinerary. It's a must-visit destination that offers a unique and unforgettable art experience.
