### A Celebration of Music: The Origins of Fête de la Musique

Fête de la Musique de Manila is an annual music festival held in the Philippines that celebrates the universal language of music. Originating from France in 1982, the festival made its way to Manila in 1994 and has since become one of the most highly anticipated cultural events in the country. Discover the roots of this extraordinary celebration and its significance in promoting music and cultural exchange.

### Music Unites: The International Reach of Fête de la Musique

Part of the global Fête de la Musique network, this festival in Manila joins over 700 cities in more than 120 countries in commemorating the universal language of music. Discover how this international movement fosters cultural exchange and unity through the power of melodies, rhythms, and harmonies.
